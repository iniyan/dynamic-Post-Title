== Description ==

Enhance your WordPress website with the "Dynamic Post Titles" plugin, a powerful tool that adds a creative twist to your post titles. With this plugin, you can provide multiple title options for each post and have them displayed dynamically, engaging your audience with fresh and captivating titles.


== Features ==

- **Title Variation:** Generate excitement and intrigue by offering multiple title options for your posts. Create a set of alternative titles, and let the plugin choose one randomly for each visitor.

- **Engage Your Audience:** Keep your content fresh and engaging. Dynamic titles capture your readers' attention, increasing the chances of them clicking on your posts and staying on your website longer.

- **Easy Setup:** The plugin seamlessly integrates with the WordPress post editor, allowing you to add and manage multiple title options effortlessly.

- **Customization:** Tailor your title options to suit your content strategy. Experiment with different wording, styles, or tones to find the best fit for your audience.

- **Ideal for Bloggers and Publishers:** Whether you run a blog, news site, or any content-based platform, "Dynamic Post Titles" adds a fresh and exciting element to your content strategy.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the "dynamic-post-titles" folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Configure the plugin by visiting the "Dynamic Post Titles" settings page in your WordPress admin panel.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How can I add multiple title options to my posts? =

To add multiple title options, go to the post editor and look for the "Dynamic Post Titles" section. You can add and manage your title options there.
